Our Menu

Good Service and Better Food

Fish Balls are very popular in Asia. Mainly they are eaten on their own, marinated, dipped in a sauce or when coming in a bowl mixed with ‘kway teow’ noodles, tofu or even rice.

Satay is the undisputed King or Queen of Asian Foods. meat on a stick roasted over charcoal, peanut sauce, chopped onions, cucumber or other veggies as well.

Chicken curry, an exotic recipe that takes us to other places like India. With wild rice, whose dark color contrasts perfectly with the yellows of the curry. A very complete dish that the whole family will like.

Spring Rolls, there are many versions of this delicious frying. We have filled them with vegetables and added soy noodles. Although other ingredients can be used, the combinations are almost endless.
